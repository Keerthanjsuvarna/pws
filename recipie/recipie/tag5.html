vegetable soup

Vegetable soup is a healthy and delicious dish that can be made in many different ways

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

8 servings

Tags : Chicken (2) Breakfast (2) Carrots (3) Food (5) juice (1)

instructions

step 1

Heat some oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, celery, and tomato paste. Cook until the vegetables have softened and the onions are translucent.

step 2

Add garlic, fennel, black pepper, salt, and red pepper flakes. Cook for one minute.

step 3

Add vegetable broth or water along with your vegetables of choice (such as potatoes, green beans, and peas). Bring everything to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let the soup cook for about 20-30 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
