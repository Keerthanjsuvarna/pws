biryani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice and Chicken Biryani is a popular Indian dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and a variety of spices such as turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The chicken is marinated in yogurt and spices before being cooked with onions, tomatoes, and other ingredients. The rice is cooked separately with whole spices like bay leaves, cinnamon sticks, and cardamom pods before being layered with the chicken mixture and cooked together until the flavors meld together.

prep time

45 min.

cook time

1.15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken (2) Breakfast (2) Carrots (3) Food (5) juice (1)

instructions

step 1

Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 30 minutes.

step 2

Cook the rice separately with whole spices like bay leaves, cinnamon sticks, and cardamom pods before being layered with the chicken mixture.

step 3

Layer the cooked chicken mixture over the rice and cook together until the flavors meld together.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups of rice

1/2 cup every masala

1 ghee and oil

1kg of chicken

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
