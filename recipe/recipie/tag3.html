mushroom tika

Mushroom tikka masala is a vegetarian version of the popular Indian dish chicken tikka masala. It’s made by marinating mushrooms in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then grilling or baking them until they’re tender and slightly charred. The mushrooms are then simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce that’s flavored with more spices like garam masala, cumin, and coriander.

prep time

35 min.

cook time

55 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Chicken (2) Breakfast (2) Carrots (3) Food (5) juice (1)

instructions

step 1

Marinate sliced mushrooms in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt for at least 30 minutes.

step 2

Grill or bake the marinated mushrooms until they’re tender and slightly charred.

step 3

Add the grilled mushrooms to a creamy tomato-based sauce that’s flavored with more spices like garam masala, cumin, and coriander and simmer for 5-10 minutes

ingredients

1 1/2 cups of mushroom

1/2 cup every masala

1 big vessel with lid and ghee

1 cup vegetables
