pani puri

Pani puri is a popular Indian street food that consists of small, crispy, hollow puris that are filled with a mixture of potatoes, chickpeas, onions, and spices. The puris are then dipped into a tangy tamarind water called pani and eaten whole in one bite.

prep time

45 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken (2) Breakfast (2) Carrots (3) Food (5) juice (1)

instructions

step 1

Make the puris by mixing semolina, wheat flour, baking soda, salt, and water together to form a stiff dough. Roll out small balls of dough and deep fry them until they puff up and turn golden brown.

step 2

Make the filling by boiling potatoes and chickpeas together with spices like cumin powder, chaat masala, and chili powder.

step 3

Make the pani by blending mint leaves, cilantro, ginger, green chili, black salt, cumin powder, tamarind paste, and water together until smooth.
