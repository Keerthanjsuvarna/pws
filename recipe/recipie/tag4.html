prawns chilly

Prawn chili is a spicy Indo-Chinese dish made with prawns that are marinated in a mixture of spices and then stir-fried with vegetables like onions, bell peppers, and green chilies. The dish is typically flavored with soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce for a tangy and spicy kick

prep time

35 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Chicken (2) Breakfast (2) Carrots (3) Food (5) juice (1)

instructions

step 1

Marinate the prawns with salt and sugar for 10 minutes.

step 2

Fry the prawns until they turn pink.

step 3

Stir-fry onions, bell peppers, and green chilies in a pan with soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and salt to taste. Add the fried prawns to the pan and stir-fry for another minute or two.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups of prawns

1/2 cup every masala and sauce

1 big vessel with lid and ghee

1 cup vegetables and vinegar

spoon

Large Pot With Lid
